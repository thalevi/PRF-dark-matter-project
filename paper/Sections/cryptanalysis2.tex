\section{Cryptanalysis}
\label{sec:cryptanalysis}
In this section, we will cryptanalyze both our $(2,3)$-constructions and our LPN-style-constructions from Section~\ref{sec:candidates}. Our constructions are parameterized using $n,m$ and $t$, such that $m > t$. The relation between $n$ and $m$ depends on the specific construction.
